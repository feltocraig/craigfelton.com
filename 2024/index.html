My Favorite Things of 2024

Welcome to my curated collection of discoveries and experiences from 2024. While not everything featured here was created or opened this year, each entry represents something I personally experienced for the first time in 2024. From remarkable restaurants and memorable dishes to captivating music and innovative products, these are the highlights that made my year special.

Favorite Restaurants Visited

COTE Korean Steakhouse • Miami, Florida A Miami outpost of the Michelin-starred New York City favorite, COTE combines the precision of a high-end steakhouse with the interactive flair of Korean BBQ. The tasting menu left such an impression that my girlfriend and I haven't stopped raving about it since our visit.
Lom Wong • Phoenix, Arizona An authentically regional Thai restaurant in Phoenix that ventures far beyond familiar dishes. With a menu showcasing hyper-local Thai specialties rarely found in the U.S., surrendering to the chef's recommendations leads to an eye-opening culinary adventure.

Velvet Belly's Beef Tartare • Rochester, New York Expertly crafted bite-sized beef tartare served atop crispy potato galettes, elevated with truffle, parmesan, and chives. This masterfully composed dish delivers such an intense moment of pleasure that the first bite triggers an involuntary smile and eye roll – a true testament to its perfection.

Favorite Cocktail Bars

Carry On • Phoenix, Arizona A very newly opened Phoenix cocktail bar that transports you to a 1960s airliner, complete with flight attendant service, window-view screens, and rumbling chairs. From the welcome drink to the signature martini hour, this immersive aviation-themed experience makes you feel like you're truly flying while enjoying craft cocktails.
Locale Firenze • Florence, Italy Locale Firenze stands among the world's top 50 cocktail bars, offering an enchanting blend of hospitality and ambiance. From the moment guests arrive, they're welcomed with a complimentary drink, setting the tone for an exceptional evening. The space captivates with its soaring ceilings, thoughtfully designed mood lighting, and lush greenery throughout. The carefully curated soundtrack completes the sophisticated yet inviting atmosphere that makes this establishment a standout in the global cocktail scene.

Favorite Movies

Dune: Part Two Dune: Part Two delivers a visually stunning cinematic experience that truly shines in IMAX format. The film's masterful use of wide-angle shots showcases the breathtaking landscapes, making the most of the large-format presentation to immerse viewers in its world. The grand scale and visual artistry make it a standout theatrical experience of 2024.

Vesuvius Challenge 2023 Grand Prize awarded The 2023 Vesuvius Challenge united a global community of experts to achieve the seemingly impossible: reading ancient Roman scrolls carbonized by Mount Vesuvius's eruption in 79 AD. Using computer vision and machine learning, teams successfully revealed text from these fragile artifacts without unrolling them, unlocking knowledge hidden for two millennia.
